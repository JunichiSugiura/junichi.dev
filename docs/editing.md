### Editing Flow

- Trim & Splice
- Add texts
- Grouping
- Add background music
  - around -35dB
- End Screen
- Color grading
  - Adjust color balance
  - Apply ambient color to the background
- Process voice audio
  - Remove noize
  - Apply complessor
  - Apply limitter
  - Adjust level (in between -12dB and -6dB)
  - Apply eq
- Export master
- Thumbnail
